Consulting Company

### Program and Policy Evaluation and Review
What makes a good program? How can we be sure these programs are working? After we review a program, how can systems-level changes be made to support individuals and communities that programs were intended to support in the first place? These are the questions we are always asking at 365 Degrees. Our evaluation services can be combined with training services to help upgrade your team’s program understanding and skillsets. 

### Workshops and Training
365 Degrees also offers specialized workshops in interpersonal skills, job readiness, communication, and mental health. 

Workshops and Training Available for Booking, suitable for all ages unless indicated otherwise: 
- Youth leadership workshops
- Mental health workshops (13+)
- Employment seeking and readiness workshops
- Community gardening workshops
- Community health program evaluation
- Community development skills training
- Introduction to evaluation skills workshop
- Asset and stakeholder mapping skills workshop
- Interpersonal communication skills workshop

Custom workshops are available following consultation. 

### Policy Advising Services
365 provides expert opinion and policy analysis, particularly as they relate to social and environmental programs. Example include accessible community-based transit planning, community gardening policy and procedures, and children’s services programs. Our technical services include KPI setting and evaluation, meaningful stakeholder engagement, and delivering training to operationalize policy measures.
