365 Degrees Consulting and Advisory Services specializes in community consultation, evaluation, strategic planning, and training services. Primary clients served by 365 are nonprofits and public sector entities looking to connect with their program participants and delivery staff.

Our key partners are nonprofit organizations, social housing providers, and community service delivery professionals. 

We deliver high-quality, customizable, affordable workshops and training to a wide variety of social and environmental service providers with a keen focus on policy and social program outcomes. We support skilled nonprofit and public sector staff as well as youth in their skill development.
